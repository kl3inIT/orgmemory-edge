# Enterprise pilot increment design

Turn the foundation into an honest, governed enterprise-pilot contract before native capture work begins.

- Make demo/native source readiness visible.
- Use a named session plus a 60-minute bound, not an unrestricted time-only search.
- Capture the reusable SOP instruction in the exported recipe.
- Carry timestamped evidence locators, decisions, exceptions, and verification flags.
- Require reviewer identity and resolved flags before export or pipe save.
- Define two-week scope, scorecard, privacy boundary, and pilot blockers.

This increment does not claim live capture or pilot readiness.
